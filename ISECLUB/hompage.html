ABOUT US

NITTE, officially NITTE (Deemed to be University), is an institute of higher education located in Yelahanka,
Bangalore, India. It is formed under the Trust of NIITE, a trust sponsored by Nitte Education Trust[1] which has established 31 institutions spread in three campuses at Nitte, Mangalore and Bangalore. The Government of India conferred the status of Deemed-to-be University[2] in June 2008.[3] The institution has been accredited with 'A' grade by the National Assessment & Accreditation Council (NAAC).

2.CODE WITH ME


Code With Me allows developers to invite guests to join them right in their IDE, to investigate issues together, review code, do pair programming, and effectively collaborate in real-time. All key IDE features such as auto-completion, smart navigation, debugging, and even terminal access are available for guests. With support for audio/video and chats, Code With Me provides a comprehensive solution that makes collaborating a smooth and rich experience. And all of the functionality is offered with end-to-end encryption
